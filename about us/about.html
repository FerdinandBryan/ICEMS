Welcome to ICEMS

Welcome to the Integrated Clearance and Event Management System (ICEMS) — a modern digital solution proudly developed by BSIT students of PUP Santa Maria Campus to revolutionize campus administrative processes.

Our Mission

To deliver a seamless, efficient, and user-friendly platform that simplifies clearance processing and event coordination.

Our Vision

To be the leading campus-wide digital ecosystem that transforms traditional paperwork into fast, transparent, and innovative solutions.

Our Services

Clearance Processing

Paperless, real-time, multi-department clearance system

Event Management

Create events, RSVP, and track attendance instantly

Real-time Notifications

Get updates via email and in-system alerts

Reports & Analytics

Generate clearance and event reports with one click
